About Julia Hester

Ms. Julia Hester, born on August 10, 1928 in Palmetto, Florida, was the daughter of Preston and Lania Hester. She was the sixth of seven children.

In 1948, Ms. Hester was married to the late Louchis Hogan, Sr. To this union, five children were born. In 1960, she was married to the late Juan Ocasio. To this union, two children were born. The family lived in Miami and New York City, where Ms. Hester continued her education at the Baby and Geriatric Career Institute of New York and the University of the State of New York. She worked at Flower Fifth Avenue Hospital in New York City until her retirement to Atlanta, where she continued to take care of her family and GlobalTech Financial, a family business.

Ms. Hester had many interests. However, above all, she loved her family, the Braves, cooking, fishing, her soaps, and the slots. She was an avid reader and sometimes read a book a week. She also loved her church. Before she became very ill, she was an active member of Sharon United Medothist Church in Dunwoody, Georgia.
